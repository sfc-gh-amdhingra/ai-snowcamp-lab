ANNUAL WELLNESS VISIT
---------------------
The Annual Wellness Visit (AWV) is covered at 100% — no copay, no deductible — once per calendar
year for all enrolled members. The AWV includes a comprehensive health risk assessment, review of
current medications, vitals, and personalized health advice. Preventive screenings ordered in
conjunction with the AWV and consistent with USPSTF guidelines are also covered at 100% with no
cost share when performed by an in-network provider.

Members are encouraged to schedule their AWV as a dedicated appointment and not combine it with
the treatment of acute conditions, which would convert the visit to a diagnostic visit and trigger
applicable cost-sharing.


PREVENTIVE SCREENINGS — COVERED AT 100%
-----------------------------------------
The following preventive screenings are covered at no cost share when performed by an in-network
provider and ordered in accordance with USPSTF guidelines:

  Colorectal Cancer Screening (Members age 45–75):
    - Colonoscopy: every 10 years for average-risk members; covered at 100%
    - Annual FIT (Fecal Immunochemical Test) or stool-based DNA test: annually as an alternative
      to colonoscopy; covered at 100%
    - If a polyp or other abnormality is found and removed during a screening colonoscopy, the
      procedure retains its preventive designation and is not reclassified as diagnostic.

  Breast Cancer Screening:
    - Annual screening mammogram for women age 40 and older; covered at 100%
    - 3D mammography (tomosynthesis) covered when ordered by an in-network provider

  Cervical Cancer Screening:
    - Pap smear every 3 years for women age 21–65 (cytology alone)
    - Pap smear with HPV co-testing every 5 years for women age 30–65 (preferred interval)
    - Covered at 100% at the recommended screening frequency


SPECIALIST VISITS
-----------------
  PPO / EPO plans:  $40 copay per specialist visit; no referral required
  HMO plans:        $40 copay per specialist visit; PCP referral required except as noted below

  Referral Waiver: No referral is required for OB/GYN visits (all plan types) or initial mental
  health and substance use disorder visits (all plan types).


MENTAL HEALTH AND SUBSTANCE USE DISORDER
------------------------------------------
Mental health and substance use disorder (SUD) benefits are provided in full parity with medical
and surgical benefits, in compliance with the Mental Health Parity and Addiction Equity Act (MHPAEA).

  - Outpatient therapy visits: $30 copay per visit
  - Inpatient psychiatric: subject to inpatient facility cost-sharing (see separate schedule)
  - Telehealth mental health visits: available at the same $30 copay as in-person visits
  - In-network provider access preferred; out-of-network benefits available at higher cost-sharing
  - Intensive Outpatient Programs (IOP) and Partial Hospitalization Programs (PHP): covered with
    prior authorization; treated as outpatient services for cost-sharing purposes
  - Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT) for opioid use disorder: covered; prescriptions subject
    to standard pharmacy benefit


PHYSICAL THERAPY
----------------
Physical therapy (PT) is covered for medically necessary conditions including post-surgical
rehabilitation, musculoskeletal injuries, and neurological conditions.

  - Member cost share: $40 copay per visit
  - Annual benefit limit: 30 visits per calendar year (in-network)
  - Prior authorization is not required for the first 12 visits
  - Prior authorization is required beginning with the 13th visit in a calendar year;
    documentation of functional improvement and an updated treatment plan must be submitted
    by the treating therapist
  - Occupational therapy and speech therapy are subject to the same visit limit and PA thresholds
    but under separate annual accumulators


EMERGENCY CARE
--------------
  - Emergency Room visit copay: $250 per visit (in-network and out-of-network)
  - The ER copay is waived if the member is admitted to the hospital directly from the ER
  - Observation status: billed as outpatient; standard outpatient cost-sharing applies; the ER
    copay does not apply when admission to observation follows the initial ER evaluation
  - Ambulance services: 20% coinsurance after deductible for medically necessary transport


DURABLE MEDICAL EQUIPMENT (DME)
---------------------------------
  - Cost share: 20% coinsurance after the annual deductible
  - Prior authorization is required for any single DME item or rental with a purchase price
    or cumulative rental cost exceeding $500
  - CPAP/APAP devices: covered with documentation of a qualifying sleep study (polysomnography
    or home sleep apnea test) and a diagnosis of obstructive sleep apnea; supplies (masks,
    tubing, filters) covered per replacement schedule
  - Orthotics and prosthetics: covered with physician order and PA; subject to plan medical
    necessity criteria


OUT-OF-POCKET MAXIMUM
----------------------
  In-network annual out-of-pocket maximum:
    Individual:  $4,000
    Family:      $8,000

  The out-of-pocket maximum accumulates all in-network copays, coinsurance, and deductible
  payments. Once the member or family maximum is reached, the plan covers 100% of in-network
  covered services for the remainder of the calendar year. Out-of-network cost-sharing
  accumulates to a separate, higher out-of-pocket maximum.
